✅ Pre-Adoption Checklist

  • Calculate monthly budget (food, vet, insurance, emergencies)
  • Measure your space and check breed size compatibility
  • Check building/apartment pet policies
  • Plan for daily walks and exercise time
  • Identify a trusted veterinarian nearby
  • Buy essentials before the dog arrives
  • Prepare a quiet space for the dog to decompress
  • Set house rules with all family members
  • Research the specific breed or mix you're considering
  • Plan for the first 72 hours — the most critical period
