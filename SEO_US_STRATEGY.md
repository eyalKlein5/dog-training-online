# 🇺🇸 US Market SEO Strategy — dog-training.online

## 1. Competitor Analysis

### 1.1 K9 of Mine (k9ofmine.com)
- **Domain Authority:** ~60 (estimate)
- **Content Strategy:** Blog-heavy with 4 pillars: Training, Breed Profiles, Food Reviews, Product Reviews
- **Revenue Model:** Affiliate links + Online Courses + Newsletter
- **Strengths:** Massive content library, regular publishing, expert contributors, strong social presence (FB, Twitter, IG, YouTube, Pinterest)
- **Weaknesses:** No animation courses, no kids-specific content, no dual-approach (positive + balanced)

### 1.2 Dunbar Academy (dunbaracademy.com)
- **Domain Authority:** ~50 (estimate)
- **Content Strategy:** Course-focused, video-heavy, Dr. Ian Dunbar brand
- **Revenue Model:** Paid courses + Free content funnel
- **Strengths:** Strong personal brand, scientific approach, video quality
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, single trainer voice, no breed-specific content, no animation

### 1.3 Spirit Dog Training (spiritdogtraining.com)
- **Domain Authority:** ~45 (estimate)
- **Content Strategy:** Course bundles, email funnel, free workshops
- **Revenue Model:** Course sales ($47-$297 bundles)
- **Strengths:** Affordable pricing, strong email marketing, refund guarantee
- **Weaknesses:** No dual approach, limited free content, no kids courses

### 1.4 Udemy / MasterClass
- **Domain Authority:** ~90 (Udemy)
- **Content Strategy:** Platform model — anyone can publish
- **Revenue Model:** Revenue share with instructors
- **Strengths:** Massive traffic, low prices ($12.99 sales)
- **Weaknesses:** Low quality control, no community, no follow-up support

  "provider": {
    "@type": "Organization",
