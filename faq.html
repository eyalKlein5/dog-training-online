No expensive gear required. You'll need a standard flat collar or harness, a 4–6 foot leash, high-value treats (small, soft, and smelly work best), and your dog's favorite toy. Optional items include a clicker (or you can use a marker word like "Yes!") and a mat or bed for settling exercises. Most of what you need is already in your home.

Many owners notice improvement in the very first session. Basic behaviors like sit, down, and focus often click within 3–5 short practice sessions. More complex behaviors — reliable recall, loose-leash walking, and impulse control — typically show real progress within 2–4 weeks of consistent practice. Every dog learns at their own pace, and the key is consistency, not speed. Our structured lesson plans keep you on track.
